Touchless interaction between humans and computers is a system that has received considerable attention in recent years with the benefit of removing barriers of physical contact. Several approaches are available to achieve mid-air interactions.

The vision-based technology of hand gesture recognition is an important part of human-computer interaction (HCI). In the last decades, the keyboard and mouse play a significant role in human-computer interaction.

Thus, we've made a program to control the volume on a device by just using some hand gestures.
